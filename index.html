About

Elicit is a browser-based tool for collecting spoken narratives from participants using picture stimuli.

Upload images, record responses, and download the audio as .wav files for analysis.

Built by Priya Prithiviraj, 2026.

③ How to use
1
Navigate images with the → arrow.
2
Press Record to begin. Press Stop when done.
3
A tick mark over the image confirms a saved recording.
4
Press Download to save all recordings.
The recording will continue across images unless manually stopped. Stop and re-record per image if you want to save as separate files.
